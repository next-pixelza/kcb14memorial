Competition Rules

Donate today, honour Kyle's legacy and stand a chance to win a Weekend Away for Two valued at R8,000.

First 20 Donors Challenge

Only the first 20 qualifying donations received will be entered into the draw.

KCB14 YOUR NAME

How To Enter

  • Donate a minimum of R200.
  • Use the payment reference KCB14 followed by your name.
  • Example: KCB14 John Smith
  • Only the first 20 donations that successfully reflect in the account will qualify.

Eligibility

  • Open to anyone aged 18 years or older.
  • Each qualifying donation of R200 or more counts as one entry.
  • Donations below R200 do not qualify for the competition.
  • There is no limit to the number of qualifying entries per person.

Winner Selection

  • The winner will be selected by a random live draw.
  • The draw will take place during the KCB14 Memorial Day event.
  • The winner does not need to be present to win.
  • The organisers will contact the winner using the details supplied with their donation.

Important Information

  • All donations support the Kyle Clayton Barnes Legacy Fund.
  • The competition closes automatically once the first 20 qualifying donations have been received.
  • The organisers reserve the right to verify all entries.
  • The organisers' decision is final.
