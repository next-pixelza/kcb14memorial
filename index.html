The Man. The Myth. The Legacy.

Kyle Clayton Barnes wasn’t just a teammate or a name on a team sheet—he was the heartbeat of our community, a force of nature on the field, and an anchor off it. His grit, unmistakable character, and unwavering passion inspired everyone who had the privilege of running alongside him.

We host this memorial day not merely to look back in sorrow, but to actively carry forward the fire he brought to every minute of the game. Every ticket bought, every partnership forged, and every donation made directly protects and supports the family and legacy he left behind.
