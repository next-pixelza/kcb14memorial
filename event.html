The Memorial Day

Celebrating 8 years of life, brotherhood, and rugby. Playing for Kyle and his beautiful children.

"He gave his life to the sport he loved. Now, we stand line-to-line to safeguard his family."

Kyle was an absolute pillar of our First Team for a number of years—a naturally loving, courageous guy who played with a ferocious heart and left everything on the field. Eight years ago, a tragic rugby injury stole Kyle from us, leaving a massive void in our club family and, most importantly, the lives of his precious children.

As we mark 8 years of life and rugby without him, we refuse to let heartbreak define his legacy. Instead, we are gathering our community for an monumental Memorial Day to celebrate his life and raise concrete financial support for his kids. This isn't just another fixture; it's a stand of unwavering brotherhood.

The 8th Minute Tribute

To commemorate 8 years since Kyle’s passing, our final home league match will feature a dedicated moment of honor. In the 8th minute of the 1st Team game, the entire stadium will rise to applaud his enduring contribution to our crest. Be in the stands early—you do not want to miss this moment.

The Final Battle: Standing Together

The stage could not be more fitting. This memorial takes place during our final home league match of the season against CPUT. It’s going to be an intense, high-stakes encounter on the pitch—exactly the kind of relentless, hard-hitting rugby Kyle thrived on.

Every ticket sold, every roar from the stands, and every contribution made throughout the day will go directly toward a secure legacy fund dedicated to supporting Kyle's children. We invite all old boys, community members, and local supporters to pack the stands, wear your club colors with pride, and show his family that they will never walk alone.
